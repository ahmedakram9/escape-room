Instructions

Welcome!

If you’ve never played an escape room before, don’t worry. This one is simple: You just need to solve the puzzles, collect the CNCEL_CODE digits and enter the final code in the Emergency Panel before time runs out.

What you need to do

  • You will complete 5 puzzles (stations).
  • Each station gives you 1 digit of the CANCEL_CODE.
  • At the end, you enter the full code into the Emergency Panel.

How puzzles work

  • Some puzzles use drag and drop (move cards into the correct order).
  • Some puzzles use typing an answer (number or result).

Hints

  • Each puzzle has 3 hints.
  • Everytime you use a hint, it costs you 30 seconds of your time.

Timer & progress

  • You have 15 minutes to complete all 5 stations.
  • The timer is always visible at the top.
  • If the timer reaches 00:00, the mission fails.
  • The progress bar shows how far you are through the stations.
