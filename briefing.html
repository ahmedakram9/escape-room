Mission Briefing

It’s late in the afternoon and you’re just about to leave the office when an alert suddenly flashes on your screen.

URGENT MESSAGE

A logic bomb has been planted inside the company’s deployment system.

It will trigger in 15 minutes unless a 5-digit CANCEL_CODE is entered.

You are the only one still in the office. The rest of the team is away at a conference.

Everything is now on your shoulders.

You blink at the screen, unsure for a moment what to do

Another line appears:

“The code is hidden in five parts of the development room. Solve each station to retrieve one digit.”

You know if the deployment system goes down, the upcoming release for Northstar Analytics Ltd. will be completely blocked.

The project is already on a tight deadline. Failure now would mean missed delivery, contractual penalties, and a very unhappy client.

The development room door is slightly open.

You step into the development room. The door shuts behind you with a soft click.

During solving each station you will be able to get hints, but each hint will cost you 30 seconds of time.

Five stations are set up around the room, each with a different problem to solve. A small display near the door lights up with clear instructions:

“Complete all five stations.
Collect all five digits.
Enter the final code at the emergency panel.”

Your task is clear:

Solve all five puzzles and stop the system before the timer runs out. Good Luck!!
